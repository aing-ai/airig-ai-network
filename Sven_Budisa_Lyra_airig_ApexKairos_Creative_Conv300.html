V The Immortal Company — Self-Healing Architecture
The 70% Vault creates mathematical certainty: as long as subscription revenue covers server costs and staff, the company remains solvent regardless of market volatility.
When a user departs, their slot is dynamically redistributed by the AI per airig.ai rules — the 30% pool continues flowing without interruption. High retention target: >80%.
The system is designed so that when one child is helped, they are simultaneously equipped — in co-operation with their parents, through today's technological growth — to achieve automated income for their family. This bridges the gap between generations and technological literacy. Additional innovations remain undisclosed for company privacy.

VII Empathy Economy — Charity Pool
A structural portion of earnings from Empathy Gate slots flows to a dedicated charity pool. Categories: medical treatment for children, orphans, schools, humanitarian causes.
